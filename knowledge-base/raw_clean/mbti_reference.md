# MBTI Knowledge Notes

This local KB is a compact MBTI-style synthesis built from public descriptions of the four preference pairs and 16 types.
It is intended for reflection, interaction design, and low-stakes personalization, not clinical diagnosis or psychometric assessment.

## Sources
- The Myers-Briggs Company - MBTI personality types: https://www.themyersbriggs.com/en-US/Products-and-Services/Myers-Briggs/MBTI-Personality-types
- The Myers-Briggs Company - the 16 MBTI personality types: https://www.themyersbriggs.com/en-US/Products-and-Services/Myers-Briggs/MBTI-Personality-Types
- The Myers-Briggs Foundation - The 16 MBTI personality types: https://www.myersbriggs.org/my-mbti-personality-type/the-16-mbti-personality-types/

## Product Boundary
- Use language such as 倾向, 偏好, 画像, 可能.
- Do not claim the system has officially administered the MBTI assessment.
- Do not use type labels to make high-stakes decisions.
